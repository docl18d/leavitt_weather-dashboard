Use this page to find the weather in a city.  You will get the weather for the day you look as well as whetjer it is a good day for UV exposure.  Additionally you will get a five day forcast.

Put the city you are looking for in the search window and then click on the search button.

A search list of cites searched will be made that you can click on for updates.  The weather will update every thirty minutes (you will need to click on the stored city in the list.)
